# Toolbox Talks Portfolio

## About

This section contains safety awareness topics and toolbox talk materials related to workplace health and safety.

## Topics Covered

- Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Working at Height Safety
- Fire Prevention and Emergency Response
- Manual Handling Safety
- Electrical Safety Awareness
- Housekeeping and Workplace Safety

## Purpose

Toolbox talks help workers understand workplace hazards, follow safe work practices, and improve safety culture.
